Our Recipes

Hot sour soup

Hot and Sour soup is a Chinese soup that’s savoury, spicy and tangy. The broth is thickened and it’s filled with mushrooms, tofu, bamboo shoots and silky egg ribbons. The flavour and textures in this soup are an addictive combination, making it a firm Chinese restaurant favourite!
Dumplings

Making homemade dumplings can seem daunting if you've never made them before. But after you learn how to make them, you'll relish the satisfaction of mastering them. Our recipe starts with a simple dough of flour, water, and salt, rolled out thinly and cut into circles. The dough is folded around a savory filling of pork, succulent shrimp, and a colorful array of veggies for incredible flavor and texture. The dumplings are pan-fried, steamed to perfection, and served with a tasty sauce of soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, sesame oil, and crushed red pepper flakes for a subtle kick of heat.

Dim Sum

Dim sum is a large range of small Chinese dishes that are traditionally enjoyed in restaurants for brunch, with a “selection of over 1,000 varieties of small-plate Chinese foods, usually meat or vegetables in dough or a wrapper that is steamed, deep-fried or pan-fried.”
